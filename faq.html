What are the Profit Tracker Critters and what can I do with them?

Profit Tracker Critters are creatures that remain on Earth and are responsible for eliminating evil creatures to protect it. By financially supporting the Profit Tracker Critters, you can benefit from a percentage of the royalty pool and access Profit Tracker Adventure.

What is Profit Tracker Adventure and how does it work?

Profit Tracker Adventure is a game based on clicking through levels and uses a pay-to-win system. It offers different levels with associated prizes that you can claim once a day. It's an opportunity to earn daily rewards and enjoy an interactive experience.

What are the daily benefits of owning a Profit Tracker Critter?

With a Profit Tracker Critter, you can receive different daily rewards linked to each level of the game. These rewards may include NFTs or other digital assets, and you can claim one reward per level once a day.

How can I claim prizes in Profit Tracker Adventure?

To claim prizes in Profit Tracker Adventure, you must submit a screenshot to Discord in the appropriate channel, showing your Wax Wallet and the prize you've won. Prizes can only be claimed once per day.

QUOKKABARAS

The Quokkabaras were among the first intergalactic species to arrive on Earth after the universal holocaust. With the arrival of new species, humans took a back seat, but the Quokkabaras extended a friendly hand, offering them protection and establishing a joint society.

BOTPODS

The BotPods are ROBOTS designed by scientists using technology discovered and shared by the Quokkabaras. Their mission is to protect and defend the Milky Way while providing assistance to the Quokkabaras in their activities.

✦ BotPods #1

Emily Johnson spearheaded the design of the initial BotPods, which served as a pioneering research prototype.

✦ BotPods #2

Motivated by the success of the BotPods, Mr. Dimitri Petrov worked hand in hand with Emily Johnson to refine their design and enhance their equipment.

SHAMANS

Shamans guide the Profit Takers with remote viewing, foreseeing the future, spiritual healing, nature communication, and protection.

SCIENTISTS

The Quokkabara welcomed the survivors Dimitri Petrov, Emily Johnson, and Santiago Martinez, who joined hands in collaboration to enhance the joint society between the Quokkabaras and other species.
